# Marketing Campaign Performance
**LOB:** HubSpot  
**Starter:** "Show me the marketing email performance from HubSpot. Which campaigns have the best open rates?"

## What this skill does
- Shows email campaigns with open rate, click rate, and send count metric cards
- Drills from emails → contact lists → individual contacts
- Supports adding/removing contacts from lists
- Allows creating new contacts and deals from the widget

## Example prompts
- "Show me marketing email performance"
- "Show me the contact lists"
- "Show me contacts in the Enterprise Prospects list"
- "Add john.smith@acme.com to the Enterprise Prospects list"
- "Create a new contact — James Pemberton, james@gtc.internal"
