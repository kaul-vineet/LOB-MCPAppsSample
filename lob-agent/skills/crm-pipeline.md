# CRM Pipeline Review
**LOB:** Salesforce  
**Starter:** "Show me the latest leads. I want to review them, update any that are qualified, and then check the opportunities pipeline."

## What this skill does
- Lists the 5 most recent Salesforce leads with status, company, and contact info
- Allows inline editing of lead status, stage, or notes from the widget
- Pivots to opportunities to review the full pipeline
- Supports creating new leads or opportunities mid-conversation

## Example prompts
- "Show me the latest leads"
- "Create a new lead for Sarah Chen at CloudBase Corp, email sarah@cloudbase.io"
- "Show me opportunities closing this quarter"
- "Update lead 00Q123 — mark as Qualified"
