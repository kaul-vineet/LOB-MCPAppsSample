# Cross-LOB Operations
**LOBs:** All  
**Starter:** "Give me a quick overview — show me open CRM leads, any critical IT incidents, and today's purchase orders."

## What this skill does
- Spans multiple LOB backends in a single conversation turn
- Correlates data across systems (e.g. a new lead + a VPN incident for their onboarding)
- Supports end-of-day operations checks across all trading posts
- Demonstrates the agent's multi-runtime routing capability

## Example prompts
- "Give me an end-of-day operations check"
- "I met a prospect — create a lead for Sarah Chen at CloudBase Corp, and raise a VPN access incident for their onboarding"
- "Check on Acme — any open leads and incidents?"
- "Show me suppliers from SAP and cross-reference with HubSpot contacts"
- "Log a lead for John at TechCorp and raise a ServiceNow request for a new laptop"
