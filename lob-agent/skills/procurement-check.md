# Procurement & Materials Check
**LOB:** SAP S/4HANA  
**Starter:** "Show me recent purchase orders from SAP and check if we have the materials in stock for order 4500000001."

## What this skill does
- Lists recent purchase orders with supplier, amount, and status
- Shows business partners (suppliers) and their details
- Browses materials master data with stock levels
- Drills into material detail cards for full specification

## Example prompts
- "Show me recent purchase orders"
- "Show me our suppliers"
- "Show me materials inventory"
- "Create a purchase order for Domestic US Supplier 1, purchasing org US01"
- "Show me the material detail for TG11"
